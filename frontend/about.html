Every year, thousands of students arrive in Tashkent facing a fragmented and often frustrating housing market. Finding a place to call home should not be the hardest part of university life.

Sigamiz connects students who already have a place with students looking for one. District, university, housing type, room count, description, phone and Telegram contacts are shown clearly.

Safety by Design

We prioritize privacy and security above all else. Here is how the platform keeps the community accountable.

my_location

Approximate Markers

Exact property locations are blurred before publication. Public map markers represent the general neighborhood, not the door.

shield_person

Telegram Verification

Communication and user identity stay connected to Telegram. Reports and repeated spam can hide listings and block abusers.
